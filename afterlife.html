Afterlife

Found Object Sculpture · 3 works · 2024

Every object around us is living out a lifespan. When that lifespan ends, it usually ends in landfills, waste plants or forgotten corners. Most objects reach that point quietly and disappear. Afterlife is about the objects that didn't stop there.
