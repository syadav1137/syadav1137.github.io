Knit-Knot-Dry · CVA Gallery · 2024

A dryer that could have been lying in a waste plant has been repurposed to get a new life. Inside it, tangled threads, wires, and metal strips sit together — the kinds of materials that make up everyday objects. By themselves they are waste. Here they are part of something different than their original purpose.

At least they get to live.

Exhibition

Knit-Knot-Dry was selected for the Juried Student Exhibition at the CVA Gallery, 2024.
